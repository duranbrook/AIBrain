- **autodb.cc**: MySQL database dump of BMW ETK — **€800 one-time + €250/update**; JSON API: €2,000 one-time or €200+/month
- This resolves the "find $50–$200 purchasable BMW ETK" question: the BMW AOS portal is free; raw SQL dump from autodb.cc is €800

- Mercedes-Benz EPC: ~$110/year (most affordable official luxury EPC subscription)
- VW Group Partslink24: ~$480/year (official non-dealer, 43 brands)
- Volvo VIDA: $210.80/year (parts-only) — best-documented pricing of any OEM
- Toyota Microcat Market: Free for independent shops
- Ford, GM, Stellantis US: Effectively dealer-only for parts EPC; no clean non-dealer path
