Three product pillars the owner cares most about:

**1. AI Technician Assistant**
Already built (iOS). Confirmed as a top priority — keep investing here.

**2. Owner Intelligence — AI Crew**
Not a single bot. A *crew* of specialized AI assistants the owner talks to.
Each crew member handles a different domain (parts, revenue, scheduling, etc.).
Feel should be conversational team interaction, not a dashboard.
**Why:** Owner wants to run the shop by talking to their AI team, not navigating menus.
**How to apply:** When designing owner-facing AI features, frame as "who on your team handles this" rather than "which module to open."

**3. Consumer-Facing Surface (two parts)**
- Vehicle history: consumers (car owners) interact with AI to understand their own service history and pull reports
- Connected ecosystem: any car's history is queryable by both shop and consumer; the agent can pull full history for any VIN
**Why:** Product wants to serve both sides — shop operators AND their customers.
**How to apply:** Consumer features are a separate product surface, not just a view inside the shop tool.
