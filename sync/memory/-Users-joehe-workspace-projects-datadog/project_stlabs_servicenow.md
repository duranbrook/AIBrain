STLabs (Standard Template Labs) is an AI-first ITSM platform founded by Amit Agarwal, Datadog's former President + CPO (13+ years). Launched March 2026, raised $49M seed from ICONIQ Growth.

**Why:** This is directly relevant to the Datadog research project — the founder left Datadog to build in the adjacent ITSM/IT automation space.

**How to apply:** When analyzing Datadog's competitive landscape or talent/leadership, note that their former #2 executive is now building in enterprise IT ops — a space that overlaps with Datadog's observability use cases.

## ServiceNow Key Numbers (FY 2025)
- Revenue: $13.28B (+21% YoY)
- Subscription gross margin: ~83.5%
- FCF margin: ~34% ($4.58B)
- cRPO: $12.85B (+25% YoY)
- Renewal rate: 98%
- Customers: 8,800 (85% of Fortune 500)
- $5M+ ACV customers: 603
- Now Assist ACV: $600M+ (targeting $1.5B+ in 2026)
- Largest acquisition: Armis $7.75B (cybersecurity)
- Revenue model: 97% subscription, 3% professional services

## STLabs Key Facts
- Founded: incubated inside ICONIQ, launched March 2026
- Funding: $49M seed
- Product: AI-native ITSM with enterprise graph (Axiom), self-service agent, operator intelligence
- Thesis: resolve tickets autonomously (not just track them); claims ~80% auto-resolution
- Direct competitor to ServiceNow's ITSM core
- ITSM market: ~$15-17B today, projected $32-46B by 2033 (15-23% CAGR)

## Competitive Context
ServiceNow acquired Moveworks ($2.85B, Dec 2025) — the leading AI overlay for ITSM. This created white space for STLabs as an independent AI-native alternative.
