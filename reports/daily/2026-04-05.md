## Top Learnings

### 1. Used car parts marketplace has massive AI opportunity gaps — particularly in VIN-based fitment and visual part identification
**Category:** Research / Product Strategy

Deep research across 15+ platforms (Car-Part.com, LKQ, eBay Motors, PartCycle, Hollander, Row52, Pull-A-Part, etc.) revealed 10 major AI/tech opportunities not yet deployed in the market. The seller workflow (vehicle acquisition → disassembly → listing → sale → shipping) has significant friction points that AI could address: automated VIN-to-parts cataloging, visual part condition grading, predictive pricing based on supply/demand, and fitment verification. Buyer experience gaps center around trust (condition uncertainty) and discovery (cross-referencing compatible parts across interchange numbers). The US/North American market is the primary focus, with EV parts emerging as a high-growth segment.

**Why it matters:** This research establishes a foundation for potential product work in the car-parts space. The findings are documented in `interview/learning/used-car-parts-marketplace-research.md`.
